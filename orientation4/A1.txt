For the example case health center, the performance measures could be:
Time spent, which would tell how long a visitor spent in the health center
Idle time, telling how much time was spent waiting
Average time, telling how long an average visitor would spend
Visitor count, telling the total count of visitors that have entered
Visitor completion count, telling how many have completed the simulation
Visitors in any time, telling how many visitors there is or could be in any given time
